Welcome to Barclays Hamburg

Hamburg is home to Barclays Consumer Bank Europe. For more than 30 years, we have been offering consumers in Germany innovative payment and financing solutions – from credit cards to instalment loans. Our well-connected offices are located in Bahrenfeld, close to the city centre. They are now home to more than 700 employees who focus on millions of our customers every day.

Job areas

Learn more about the different job families we recruit into in Hamburg.

Customer Service

Customer Service

Our Customer Service Teams provide an end-to-end service to all of our customers including customer on-boarding, customer care, complaints handling, fraud/chargeback, financial crime, collections & recoveries for all Barclays Germany product lines.
In collaboration with our technology teams, they’re constantly working make our customer services smarter, easier and more user friendly.

Information Technology & Digital Transformation

Information Technology & Digital Transformation

Our Tech and Transformation Teams are core experts for all our IT solutions (e.g. Barclays customer app, website, internal systems, soft- and hardware) and divided into the areas “RUN” and “CHANGE”.
On one hand they ensure that all colleagues and customers can work with our IT solutions (“RUN”). On the other, they are continuously working on digitalising and optimising our processes, as well as IT and product solutions (“CHANGE”).

Sales & Marketing

Sales & Marketing

Our Sales & Marketing Department consists of the Brand, Customer Engagement, Digital Experience and Sales teams. We create solutions that are customer focused and innovative.
This includes the development of brand and communication activities, the constant enhancement of our digital distribution channels – also in co-operation with 3rd party distribution - to improve our sales success partners, the deepening of the relationships with our existing customers, the continuous development of our Online Banking and Banking App and the end-to-end optimisation of our customer experience.

Communications, Compliance, Credit Risk, Finance, Human Resources & Legal

Communications, Compliance, Credit Risk, Finance, Human Resources & Legal

Supporting functions such as Communications, Compliance, Credit Risk, Finance, Human Resources & Legal help all the other departments of our bank in their daily business and with the implementation of projects.
They are important components in almost all company processes and contribute essentially to our company’s culture and objectives.

Product Management & Strategic Analytics

Product Management & Strategic Analytics

Our Product Management Teams drive customer focused and innovative product solutions that are of immense importance for the commercial success and the ongoing digitalisation of our bank.
These solutions contain all of our products and services such as credit cards, instalment loans, instalment purchases and call money investments.
Our Strategic Analytics Team develops, maintains and works on strategies to optimise profit within the business objectives and risk appetite constraints.

Benefits

Discover the different benefits we offer to colleagues in Barclays Europe.

Arena tickets

Arena Tickets

As a name sponsor of the Barclays Arena, we offer each of our employees the opportunity to receive up to 8 tickets per season for various events.

Employee credit

Employee Credit

As a Barclays employee, you can apply for credit cards that are exempt from annual fees or take advantage of installment loans with a fixed special interest rate.

Community days

Community Days

Each employee receives two working days per year for selected community activities. For example, we are strongly committed to exceptional social and sustainability projects and initiatives.

Personal development

Personal Development

Barclays offers employees the opportunity to further develop according to their individual strengths. For example, during a varied training programme or an internal internship in another area. Solutions can be found for different situations with our flexitime model and part-time employment offers, as well as sabbaticals. In addition, we actively promote international cooperation with colleagues from different countries.

Flexible budget

Flexible Budget

Barclays offers a flexible budget for individual needs, which is either paid out directly with the salary or can be used for specific purposes (e.g. parking, capital-forming benefits, deferred compensation for retirement provision).

Company pension

Company Pension

Barclays pays a high share for a company pension plan; only a small personal contribution is mandatory. Barclays also subsidises employee-funded pension plans in the form of deferred compensation. This means tax advantages compared to payments into private life or pension insurances.
